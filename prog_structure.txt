find_corr_delta_matrix
find a 3-D matrix with three axis. 
Aixs one: a vector of industry index
Axis two: a vector of industry index (exactly same as axis one)
Axis three: delta-t (the time shifted between two timeseries)

The meaning of a point in the matrix: x = A('Finance', 'Energy', 66) :
point x in the matrix holds an numerical value, which represents the correlation between two timeseries(Finance index close price, and Energy index close price)
